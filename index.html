Our product

Meraqi

Meraqi is an AI-assisted design platform that helps anyone create professional, on-brand images, video, 3D and text content, and distribute it across channels.

It comes in two forms: an AI-powered SaaS platform on the web, and a self-hosted deployment that runs entirely inside an organization's own network, up to fully air-gapped, for the highest levels of security. That combination defines a new category of product, built for a critical part of the economy that AI tools have so far left unserved: government, defense, healthcare, finance, legal, and any team whose content can never leave its own environment.

The scale and scope are practically boundless. A personalized greeting card. A book cover. An ad campaign, or a clip for social media. Type a prompt to create an image, then add a QR code that links anywhere. A wedding invitation with directions to the venue, the map link turned into a scannable code right on the card. Share a link to your product or marketing page and get a video for the big announcement. Load your meeting notes, a voice recording and your financial statements, and get back a presentation ready for new clients. And the list goes on.
